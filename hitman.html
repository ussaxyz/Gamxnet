Hitman Absolution Mobile

For Android and iOS

Size

2.4 GB (Installer) 4GB+ (Required Space)

Price

Free

Android Compatible

Android 10.0+, 4GB RAM, Snapdragon 700 series+

iOS Devices Required

iPhone 8 or newer, iOS 14.0 or later

Installation Guide

Android
iOS
  • Download the Hitman Absolution APK from the button above
  • Go to Settings > Security > Enable "Unknown Sources"
  • Locate and install the APK file on your device
  • Launch the app to start downloading the OBB data files
  • Wait for the synchronization and enjoy the mission!
  • Download the IPA file using the iOS download button
  • Use AltStore or Sideloadly on your PC to install the file
  • Connect your device and sign with your Apple ID
  • Trust the developer profile in Settings > General
  • Open Hitman and download the internal game assets

Features

  • Instinct Mode: See the world through the eyes of Agent 47
  • Disguises: Blend in and deceive your enemies to reach targets
  • Environmental Kills: Use your surroundings for "accidental" eliminations
  • HD Graphics: Console-quality visuals optimized for mobile screens
  • Contract Mode: Play custom-made hits and challenges
