SnowRunner Mobile

For Android and iOS

Size

2.5 GB (Installer) 5GB+ (Required Space)

Price

Free

Android Compatible

Android 10.0+, 4GB RAM, Snapdragon 700 series+

iOS Devices Required

iPhone 11 or newer, iOS 14.0 or later

Installation Guide

Android
iOS
  • Download the SnowRunner APK using the link above
  • Ensure "Install from Unknown Sources" is enabled in settings
  • Install the APK and wait for the OBB assets to extract
  • Launch the game and allow the terrain physics to synchronize
  • Start your off-road mission across the wilderness!
  • Download the IPA file via the iOS download button
  • Sideload the application using AltStore or Sideloadly
  • Trust the developer certificate in Settings > General
  • Open SnowRunner and download the high-resolution textures
  • Enjoy the most realistic mud and snow physics on mobile!

Features

  • Extreme Environments: Conquer mud, torrential waters, snow, and frozen lakes
  • Huge Roster: Unlock and drive 40 unique vehicles from brands like Ford and Chevrolet
  • Advanced Physics: Experience the most realistic terrain simulation on a mobile device
  • Open World: Explore vast, untamed maps in total freedom
  • Contract Missions: Complete dozens of challenging missions across an interconnected world
